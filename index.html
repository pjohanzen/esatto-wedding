FAQ

Common Questions

What suit colors do you have?

+

We have hundreds of fabric options in virtually any color you can imagine, along with full control over design details. We often make wedding suits in red, burgundy, tan, browns, and greens in addition to traditional blue and black wedding suits and tuxedos.

How long does a standard order take?

+

A standard order typically takes about 4 weeks for production and delivery. Rush options are available in 2 weeks or less.

What does the client process look like?

+

We start with an initial fitting to take measurements, choose fabrics, and complete payment. Your order is delivered in about 3-4 weeks, then you return for a second fitting. If any alterations or remakes are needed, we cover the cost until you're happy.

How far in advance should I plan?

+

Ideally allow 6-8 weeks for custom suits and 2-3 months for full bespoke options.
