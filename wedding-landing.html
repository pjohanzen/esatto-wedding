FAQ

Common Questions

How far in advance should I order my wedding suit?

+

We recommend at least 3-4 months before your wedding for custom suits, and 4-5 months for full bespoke. This gives us plenty of time for fittings and any adjustments.

Can my groomsmen be measured remotely?

+

Yes! For out-of-town groomsmen, we have a process to get accurate measurements remotely. Contact us for details.

What's the difference between Custom and Full Bespoke?

+

Full Bespoke includes unlimited fittings until the fit is exactly how you want it and takes 10-12 weeks. Custom includes one fitting with alterations covered and takes 4-5 weeks.

Do you offer group discounts for wedding parties?

+

Yes, we offer special pricing for wedding parties. Reach out to discuss your group size and we'll put together a custom package for you.
