FAQ

Common Questions

How customizable are your suits?

+

We offer hundreds of fabric options in virtually any color you can imagine, along with full control over design details.

How long does a standard order take?

+

A standard order typically takes about 4 weeks for production and delivery. Rush options are available in 2 weeks or less.

What does the client process look like?

+

We start with an initial fitting to take measurements, choose fabrics, and complete payment. Your order is delivered in about 3-4 weeks, then you return for a second fitting. We cover any needed alterations or remakes.

How far in advance should I plan?

+

Ideally allow 6-8 weeks for custom suits and 2-3 months for full bespoke options.

Do you offer group discounts for wedding parties?

+

Yes, we offer special pricing for wedding parties. Reach out to discuss your group size and we'll put together a custom package for you.
